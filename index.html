It's not just a pizza...

Every ingredient is bought locally, never frozen, and every meal is made with love.

We buy local produce daily. Our sourdough and all sauces are made in house. Our cheese and milk is purchased from Parks Brothes Dairy Farms located in Northern Oregon, and our meat is purchase and hand-raised by Zebrowski Farms in Northern Washington. Know every meal you order, and every bite you eat is responsibly made and cultivated with tender, love, and care.
